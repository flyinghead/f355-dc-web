YS MESSAGE


Having released "F355 Challenge" last July, I am very happy that many people, including professional drivers are enjoying this game. At last, we are able to present this game to the homes of Dreamcast fans. In porting this game from the arcade version, we have added many new ways of play, in addition to the simulator element which was a strong characteristic of the original title. This game can be enjoyed casually by users who are playing games for their first time, and of course provides satisfaction to racing game enthusiasts. I hope motor-sports fans will be happy with this title, as they were with the arcade version, and in addition to that, I hope many game fans will be happy also.

August, 2000
Producer of "F355 Challenge - Passione Rossa"
Yu Suzuki
