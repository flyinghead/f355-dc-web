How to register for the Internet Ranking


Please follow the instructions below to register

1. Save your game data into your VM
After playing "F355 Challenge", select "Save Game" from the Main Menu, and save your game data into your VM



2. Register your personal data
If you are registering for the first time, you need to first register your personal data. Insert a VM into your Dreamcast, and go to the Player Data screen. Click on the "Personal Data Registration Form" button. Fill out the form and register.

Go to the Player Data screen ---> GO



3. Register for the Internet Ranking
Insert a VM into your Dreamcast, and go to the Player Data screen. Click on the "Ranking Registration" button.

Go to the Player Data screen ---> GO



Follow the steps above to register your data.
